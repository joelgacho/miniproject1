Criminal Law

We deal with all criminal matters. We negotiate, defend, intervene, and pursue.

In matters of marriage annulment, nullification, and divorce recognition, we represent clients.

Intellectual Property Law

The registration of patents, trademarks, and other intellectual property is something we help with. We pursue legal action against anyone who violate intellectual property rights. Both domestic and foreign clients are represented by us.

For injuries caused by medical misconduct, demand compensation. Make a malpractice claim.

We assist candidates during campaign, election, and canvassing. If one has lost, we represent one in protest.
